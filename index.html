Cloud Project Proposal

Aesthé has been engineered from the ground up as an online store to provide a seamless and tailored buying process. The platform operates on Microsoft Azure and Amazon EC2, which, as Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), leads to maximized scalability, security, and customer performance.
